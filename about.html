Who We Are

Why INSARA Foundation was created

INSARA Foundation is a newly established nonprofit organization based in Mianwali, Punjab, Pakistan. Our mission is to serve humanity through compassion, transparency, and sustainable community development.

We are beginning our journey with a clear purpose: to stand alongside underprivileged children, families, and vulnerable communities. We don't have a long history to point to yet — what we have is a founding team, a plan, and a genuine commitment to build something lasting, one honest step at a time.
