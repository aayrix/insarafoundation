Our Vision
A future where every child, family, and community has access to opportunities that lead to education, good health, and a better quality of life.
Our Aim

Every bright mind deserves a chance to learn

Our aim is to support the education of students who have good brains but, due to financial constraints, are unable to go to school and colleges.

Too many talented children in Mianwali are forced into labour before they ever get a fair start. INSARA Foundation exists to help deserving students stay in school and build a future their abilities deserve.

Who We Are

A newly established foundation, rooted in Mianwali

INSARA Foundation is a newly established nonprofit organization based in Mianwali, Punjab, Pakistan. Our mission is to serve humanity through compassion, transparency, and sustainable community development.

We are beginning our journey with a clear purpose: to stand alongside underprivileged children, families, and vulnerable communities, one honest step at a time.
