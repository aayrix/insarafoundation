Who We Are

A newly established foundation, rooted in Mianwali

INSARA Foundation is a newly established nonprofit organization based in Mianwali, Punjab, Pakistan. Our mission is to serve humanity through compassion, transparency, and sustainable community development.

We are beginning our journey with a clear purpose: to stand alongside underprivileged children, families, and vulnerable communities, one honest step at a time.
